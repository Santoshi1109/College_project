About ZooSphere

ZooSphere is more than just a zoo — it is a living sanctuary where wildlife, technology, education, and conservation come together to protect nature for future generations. Established with a vision to bridge the gap between humans and wildlife, ZooSphere provides a safe, natural, and ethical environment for animals while inspiring visitors to care deeply about the planet. Spread across vast green landscapes, ZooSphere is home to over 200 species of mammals, birds, reptiles, and aquatic life. Every habitat is carefully designed to closely resemble the animals’ natural surroundings, ensuring their physical and emotional well-being. Our expert team of veterinarians, wildlife rangers, and conservationists work around the clock to monitor animal health, rescue injured wildlife, and rehabilitate endangered species.

Our Purpose

ZooSphere is founded on a strategic and ethical commitment to wildlife conservation, environmental stewardship, and responsible education. We operate at the intersection of science, sustainability, and public engagement to create a progressive model of modern wildlife management.
